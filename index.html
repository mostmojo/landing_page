The Forum

An annual event organized by the Cambridge Blockchain Hub and Bradfield Centre to promote the use of blockchain solutions that are impactful to industry, society, and the growing innovation ecosystem in Cambridge.

Purpose

Bringing together various stakeholders across industry, government, academia, students to engage in dialogue around emerging technologies, their business models, impact, and to immerse themselves in DLT and blockchain through hands on demos to inform and explore new and novel applications of the technology.

Annual Event

The Cambridge Blockchain Forum will take place November 23rd, 2018 9:30AM-18:30PM at The Bradfield Centre in Cambridge and will include speakers from Swisscom Blockchain, Hashgraph, London Stock Exchange Group and many others.
